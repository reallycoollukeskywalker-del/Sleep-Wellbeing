Health, wellbeing, and student life

Sleep shapes how students focus, feel, and function.

This website turns my research, tracking process, and reflection into a clear guide that explains why sleep matters and how students can improve it in realistic ways.

    Why sleep matters

    One habit, three major effects.

    My project focused on the everyday outcomes that students notice most: focus in school, emotional balance, and overall wellbeing.

    Project foundation

    From personal experience to a useful final product.

    I chose sleep because it affects energy, concentration, and mood, yet students often treat it like something they can ignore. This project connects to the global context of identities and relationships through health and wellbeing.

    Research and evidence

    What the science says about student sleep.
